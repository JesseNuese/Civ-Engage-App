Civility is an app built to make participating in the democratic process as easy as possible by providing you with pertinent information about your elected officials.


In today's mass media landscape, with thousands of virtual voices calling out to you, confirming your biases, and overloading your timeline, Civility aims to be an easy to use resource for cold hard facts.


We offer tools that help you find out where to cast your ballot, who represents you in the House of Representative and the Senate, and who are your Representatives biggest political donors.
